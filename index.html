I'm a designer and developer based of Greater Vancouver area

My professional intrests reside in User Experience Design, Responsive Web Design, Front end development for Android and Web. I am working towards creating meaningful & realistic work that attempts to reach people in their everyday lives, as well as purpose-driven design. White space—not just because it looks clean, minimal, or trendy—but because it works specifically in the context of the given product, experience, or audience that it's being exposed to. With any new tool, I first enjoy learning it well. This allows me to start a project feeling confident, and in my element to do detailed, honest work.

Majority of my projects have been academic, with the exception of a freelance project. I am eager for the opportunity to apply my knowledge and skills to industry, to learn and grow, to become a better designer, developer and thinker for my future self.
